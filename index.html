About Dicers Royale

Get ready for Dicers Royale! A fast and exciting battle game where your dice decide your fate.

How to Play?

It's simple: On each turn, you roll the dice! The results you get will let you attack your opponents, defend yourself, or use special powers. Think fast and use your rolls to SMASH the other players in the arena.

How to Win?

By being the last Dicer standing! You have to eliminate all your rivals. Use your dice wisely to attack and survive the others' attacks. The last player remaining at the end of the battle wins the game!

In summary: Roll the dice, smash your opponents, and be the last survivor to win!
